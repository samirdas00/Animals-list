  1. Explore More about Some Common Animals

Names of Animals About it
Alligator Alligators are large reptiles that live in the water and have a pointed mouth. They have large jaws and a sharp set of teeth. They belong to the Alligatoridae family.
Arctic fox Arctic foxes are also known as polar foxes. They are native to the Arctic region. They have a white, thick fur to keep them warm.
Buffalo Buffalos, also known as bisons, are herbivorous animals. These animals can be both domesticated as well as wild.
Chameleon Chameleons are highly specialised reptiles that are known to change colour for camouflage. There are 202 species of chameleons around the world.
Dinosaurs Though extinct, dinosaurs were reptiles that lived on the Earth during the Triassic period. There were different species of dinosaurs that roamed the Earth millions of years ago.
